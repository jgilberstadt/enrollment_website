ECP Registry

Extracorporeal Photopheresis (ECP) for the Management of Progressive Bronchiolitis Obliterans Syndrome (BOS)

in Medicare-Eligible Recipients of Lung Allografts

 Footer

Have you been diagnosed with lung transplant rejection? If so, you may be a perfect fit to participate in a study of Extracorporeal Photopheresis (ECP), a new treatment for rejection. In the ECP Registry Study, national expert physicians are collecting information on the use of ECP in patients nationwide in order to better understand the overall effectiveness of this treatment and to identify any factors that predict which patients benefit from this therapy.
